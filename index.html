Brief Introduction

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong, fortunately advised by Prof. Jia Pan. Now I am working on robot learning approaches, especially imitation learning, for manipulation tasks.

Before that, I received the M.S. and B.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering at the School of Vehicle and Mobility from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2023 and 2020, respectively. At THU, I was supervised by Prof. Shengbo Li who leads iDLab, working on reinforcement learning applied in autonomous driving. I also collabrated with Prof. Jianyu Chen, who leads the Intelligent System and Robotics Lab at Institute of Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University. During my time as a Master student, I was interested in reinforcement learning (RL) combined with optimal control, especially safe RL (where agents should not violate certain state constraints during and/or after the learning process), and their applications to autonomous driving and robotics.
